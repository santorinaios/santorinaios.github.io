Christodoulos Santorinaios

Welcome! I am a 2nd year PhD student in the Department of Informatics at the Athens University of Economics and Business, where I am a member of the CS Theory group. I am very fortunate to be advised by Vangelis Markakis. My research is supported by an Archimedes AI PhD fellowship.

Previously, I received a MSc in Data Science and Machine Learning from the National Technical University of Athens and a Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the same institution. During my undergraduate studies, I was a member of CoReLab, fortunate to be advised by Dimitris Fotakis.

I am broadly interested in Theoretical Computer Science and its interplay with Game Theory, Optimization and Economics. Recently, I have been working on Fair Division, Learning in Games and Mechanism Design with Predictions.
